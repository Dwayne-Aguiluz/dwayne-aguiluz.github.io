<h3>What is the most important thing I learned from the event? </h3> <br>
<p>
The most important thing I learned is to always enjoy the game at which I love no matter the situations, win or loss, and my teammates and coach teached this to me.
</p><br>

<h3> How can I apply what i have learned in real life situations? </h3> <br>
<p>
I can apply these things in my future games and even in my academics, to enjoy always the journey in success and focusing in my goals.
</p><br>

<h3> Did I actively participate in the event? How? </h3> <br>
<p> Yes, I did so by representing our school in the Basketball 5v5 and achieving the 1st runner up in this game.
</p><br>

<h3> If i were to teach this topic/subject to a classmate, how would I explain it? </h3> <br>
<p> I would tell all the things that happened while I was in the cluster meet and the process on how our team reached the finals and how we stripped the 1st runner up despite the challenges and setbacks.

<h3> Why is it important to have an event (per subject)? Explain your answer. </h3> <br>
